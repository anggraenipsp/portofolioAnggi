Nyurat


We created a website called "Nyurat" to improve communication and data management for an organization. It allows for mail management, data recording, and communication within the organization, as well as providing resources like FAQs and customer service contact.

Three team members are responsible for analyzing user needs, designing business processes, creating pseudocode and implementing and testing to ensure the project functions correctly and meets all requirements. Our team guides the project from the initial analysis phase to successful completion.

We faced challenges due to limited team members and starting from scratch, so we divided tasks evenly among team members. However, we still faced challenges with time management due to a short project timeline and team members' other commitments. To overcome these challenges, we had to be efficient with our time to meet deadlines and deliver a high-quality product.
